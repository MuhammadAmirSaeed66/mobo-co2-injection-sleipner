The economic objective is a transparent representative cost index in 2024 EUR per metric tonne of injected CO2. The capture, transport, and storage coefficients are the midpoints of the ranges reported in the European Commission JRC 2024 CCUS status report. The sensitivity analysis recalculates objective values and Pareto metrics for all evaluated designs across the full published ranges. It is a post-hoc robustness analysis conditional on the sampled designs, not a complete BO re-optimization under every economic scenario.
